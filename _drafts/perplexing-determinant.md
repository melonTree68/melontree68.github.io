Everybody saw such a definition of the determinant of a matrix $A\in\R^{n,n}$ before in class, often at the beginning of his linear algebra journey.

$$
\det A=\sum_{\sigma\in\text{perm(n)}} (-1)^{\sgn(\sigma)}
$$
